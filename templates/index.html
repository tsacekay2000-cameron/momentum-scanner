🎯 Score (0-100)

What it is: Overall momentum score combining all KPIs.

Why it matters: Higher score = stronger momentum potential. Look for 60+.

Categories: Momentum (30%), Liquidity (20%), Volatility (20%), Structure (15%), Catalyst (15%)

💰 Price & % Change

What it is: Current stock price and percentage move from today's open.

Why it matters: Shows how much the stock has moved today. Green = up, Red = down.

Target: Look for +10% or more for strong momentum plays.

📊 RVOL (Relative Volume)

What it is: Today's volume compared to the 20-day average. Example: 5x means 5 times normal volume.

Why it matters: High RVOL = unusual activity, often precedes big moves.

Target: 5x+ is explosive. 10x+ can lead to trading halts.

🎈 Float

What it is: Number of shares available for public trading (in millions).

Why it matters: Lower float = easier to move. Low float + high volume = potential squeeze.

Target: Under 20M is "low float" territory. Under 10M is very explosive.

📈 Volume

What it is: Total shares traded today.

Why it matters: Confirms the move is real. High volume = strong conviction from traders.

Tip: Compare to float - if volume exceeds float, the entire float rotated!

💵 Dollar Volume

What it is: Price × Volume. Total dollar value traded.

Why it matters: Shows liquidity. $20M+ means you can actually trade it without slippage.

Warning: Low dollar volume = hard to enter/exit positions.

📍 VWAP (Volume-Weighted Average Price)

What it is: Average price weighted by volume throughout the day.

Why it matters: Traders use VWAP as support/resistance. Above VWAP = bullish.

Strategy: Strong stocks hold above VWAP all day. Bounces off VWAP = buy signal.

📰 News

What it is: Stock has recent news/catalyst.

Why it matters: The "why" behind the move. FDA approval, earnings, upgrades, etc.

Types: FDA (strongest), Earnings, Analyst Upgrades, Press Releases (weakest)

⚠️ Halt Risk

What it is: Stock might get halted due to extreme volatility.

Why it matters: Trading halts freeze all activity. Can gap up or down when resumed.

Formula: Low float + RVOL 5x+ = halt risk. Can be opportunity or danger.

🎓 Trading Strategy Tips

Morning Gappers: Stocks up 10%+ premarket with news. Watch for continuation or fade.

Mid-Day Breakouts: Stocks breaking HOD (high of day) on volume. These can run hard.

Low Float Squeeze: Float under 20M + RVOL over 5x + Above VWAP = potential squeeze.

Risk Management: Always use stop losses. These are volatile plays. Never risk more than 1-2% of account per trade.
